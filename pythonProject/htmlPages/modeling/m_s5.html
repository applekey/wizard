CLEAN UP

Start by selecting a small area within the popiteal region as marked during the scanning process. When ready to proceed, press A square will be created at the site of your selection. Use the ‘grow squares’ to Increase the width (green square) to match that of the residual limb and the height (red square) to slightly above the trim line as marked on the residual limb and to the bottom edge of the marked popiteal region. Rotate the camera until you have the limb and square in profile and then use the rotation arrow (blue) and possibly the blue and red arrows to position the top and bottom edges of the square so that it sits on a plane with the calf area of the residual limb. When ready press Drag the brush over the now partially transparent square and press the left mouse button to fill in material between the residual limb and the square. Continue until the space is entirely filled. When ready press ‘complete build up’ to finish the process.
