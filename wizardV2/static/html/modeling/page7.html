In step 7
You will attach the Connector to the Socket, to provide a connection point for the standardized red-cross post and foot assembly. Most of this step is automated. You will only need to indicate where the Connector should approximately be positioned, using a mesh selection.

Begin Selection Tool

Click the arrow to begin the Selection Tool

Select Area

Using the selection tool that is now active, mark the region on the bottom of the socket where the Connector should be attached

When you are finished, click the arrow to position the gizmo.

Position Connector

If the Connector is positioned correctly relative to the Socket, continue to the next step. Otherwise, use the arrows to translate it to the right spot.

Position Cutting Plane

We will now cut away part of the outer shell of the Socket, which will allow us to automatically connect the Socket and Connector.

If the plane position is suitable, continue to the next step. Otherwise, use the arrows to position it, and then continue.

Finished
